Frequently Asked Questions


Q: I entered the wrong mobile number while booking. Can I get my Uticket on a different number?

Sure you can. Please enter your queries in the contact us page or contact our call center and our executive will send you the UTicket on your desired number.


Q: Do I need to sign up for booking?

Yes! You will be asked to sign up to book your tickets! By clicking "Register" button, you can sign up and book your tickets.


Q: Do I need to have an account for Checking availability of buses?

No, It is not mandatory. You can avail yourself of our checking availability page without having an account.


Q: While checking the availability, Can I View the routes of the buses?

Yes, we have an exclusive option for viewing the routes of the buses. You can avail this option by clicking on the "view route" option present in our checking availability page.


Q: To create an account, what should be my username?

The username must be your phone number.


Q: What are the key features of ULAA?

Ulaa helps you reduce the waiting time in bus stops by offering the possiblity of checking availability of buses for a particular destination within Coimbatore. Ulaa enables you to choose the destination, and book your bus ticket with just a few clicks.
